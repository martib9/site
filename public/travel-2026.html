A brutal, dog-first comparison of the cities we discussed for a relaxed 29 Dec–3 Jan trip from Cyprus via Venice or Milan. Scores are built around the actual trip you described: short dog walks, good food, coffee, cozy streets, light art, birthdays, and a calm New Year.

How to read this

Cost assumption

Estimated per couple, excluding flights, for 5 nights. Open the pop-up for the full breakdown by accommodation, food, cafes, restaurants, transport, museums, gifts, and pet extras.

Dog assumption

Dachshund logic matters: flat streets, short loops, low stairs, low fireworks exposure, easy station access, and a comfortable apartment are more important than “prettiest place on Instagram.”

Prepared 2026-06-19. Treat transfer times and costs as planning ranges; check exact 29 Dec 2026 and 3 Jan 2027 flights/trains before booking.

What the estimate covers

Per couple, excluding flights, for 29 Dec–3 Jan / 5 nights. This assumes a shared pet-friendly 2-bedroom apartment for 4 adults + 2 dogs and a relaxed, non-luxury trip style.

Accommodation share Half of a pet-friendly 2-bedroom apartment for 4 adults + 2 dogs, 5 nights. €550–900
Food at home Groceries, market food, breakfast supplies, wine, snacks, and cooking 2–3 dinners at home. €180–300
Cafes / brunch / casual lunches Coffee, pastries, casual lunches, aperitivo, and easy daytime food. €180–300
Restaurants 2–3 normal meals out plus one nicer birthday-ish dinner, not luxury tasting menus. €250–450
Local transport Airport-to-city trains/buses, local trains, and occasional taxi if useful. €70–180
Museums / light getaways 1–2 museum or art visits, or one small day trip. €50–180
Small gift shopping Books, food gifts, dog treats, ceramics, small souvenirs, or local products. €150–300
Pet extras Accommodation pet fee, cleaning surcharge, and small dog supplies. €50–120

Not included

  • Flights, unless you include them separately at around €800 for 2 people + dog.
  • Luxury hotels, New Year gala/tasting menus, private transfers, car rental, emergency vet costs, new dog carriers, vaccines/documents, or heavy shopping.
  • Airline pet fee, unless it is already inside your flight estimate.

Rule of thumb

  • Most sensible cities: around €2,150–3,200 per couple all-in with flights.
  • Como/Cernobbio and Bressanone: around €2,500–3,800 per couple all-in.
  • Ortisei/Cortina: easily €3,100–5,300+ per couple all-in.
